Currently, the City of Evanston does not allow for residents to recycle batteries. That is why at ETHS, this coming school year, people will be able to drop off their battteries every other Saturday from 9 am - 3 pm. When poeple drop off their batteries, we will have a team of student volunteers ready to take the batteries, tape them, and sort them. From there, we will load the taped and sorted batteries to a battery seller. In accordance to a new Illinois law, all battery distributers now must take in batteries and recycle them. This will allow for the public to easily recycle batteries safely and allow for batteries to not sit in landfills and potentially spark a fire.
